About the site


Biller.io is a billing site created by a group of engineering students to make generating, and paying bills much easier. Billing is often a hard, and a tedious process,especially for people with jobs and other ventures. The need for online billing has increased from COVID 19 and since the world is going more and more cashless, billing too is going online. We intend to make our customer's lives easier with this site and by eliminating paper, make this world a better place to be.
